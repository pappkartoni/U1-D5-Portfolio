About me

Hello! My name is Florian but eveyone usually just calls me Flo. I am born and raised in Hamburg, Germany and that's where I still choose to live today. From an early age on I have always been fascinated by everything technical and I always had the desire to understand how those things work. Turns out it's mostly numbers and I had a knack for those! That's why I went on to study mathematics. I got my Bachelor's degree in 2015 and decided I want to work in software development of some kind. With tons of programming languages and fields of work to choose from I eventually landed on web development. I just wanted to leave my mark on the everchanging landscape that is the world wide web.

I started with a small startup company and eventually went on to work for a large German publisher. Having seen both ends of the corporate spectrum I came to the conclusion that I want to do my own thing so to speak. Whether that turns out to be freelancing or creating my own web development company remains yet to be seen!
